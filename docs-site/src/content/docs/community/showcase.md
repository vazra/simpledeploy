Want your project listed here? Open a PR adding an entry to this page. We accept hobbyists, indie founders, internal tools, agencies, anything where SimpleDeploy is doing real work.

## Template

```md
### Project name
<one-line description>

- **What runs on SimpleDeploy**: services + roles
- **Scale**: e.g., 1 VPS, 8 apps, ~1k req/min
- **Why SimpleDeploy**: one sentence
- **Link**: https://example.com (optional)
```

_Be the first. Open a PR._

## How to participate

1. Fork [github.com/vazra/simpledeploy](https://github.com/vazra/simpledeploy).
2. Edit `docs-site/src/content/docs/community/showcase.md`.
3. Add your entry above this section.
4. Open a PR. Maintainers review weekly.

We do not require paid sponsorship and we do not surface ads. The bar is "this is a real working deployment that someone might learn from".

## Use SimpleDeploy at your company?

Even an internal mention helps. Star the repo. Tell us about your stack in [Discussions](https://github.com/vazra/simpledeploy/discussions). Write a blog post and we will link to it from the [Blog](/blog/).
